ABOUT US

Paper Bridges

Studies estimate that 140 million children are neglected globally. We believe that communication is the cornerstone of all human relationships, and it is one of many important factors to child development that is lacked for neglected children. Although nothing can replace the critical role parental love constitutes in a child’s life, we aim to be that bridge of empathy and love.

We are the Paper Bridges Project at North Carolina. Paper Bridges Corp is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in the United States, which qualifies us to give out volunteering hours. Volunteer hours will be given once you have submitted your 100-500 words letters, and will be corresponding to the amount of letters you wrote. If you need us to verify your volunteering hours or have any questions, please submit a response form to us. Together, we write and deliver letters to orphans around the world. Our team hopes to construct a bridge to the hearts of children in orphanages of strength, encouragement, and consolation; all done in the hopes that one day, they will be able to construct magnificent bridges of their own.

Write Letters

Help us write letters to orphans around the world in orphanages like Orphan's Hope, CASA Guatemala, and Our Little Roses. Submit it in the form below that direct to our project's email. We will proofread and word check it for you. Be encouraging! Let the orphans know that you are looking out for them. Each letter should be from 100 to 500 words. A few suggestions:

Tell them a little about yourself: Where are you from? What are some of your hobbies? What do you do on a typical day?

Talk about topics that they can relate to such as (but not limited to) school, sports, friends, and art.


Still not sure what to write? Here are some examples

>

Dear friend, How are you? I hope you find this letter cheer you up. I am looking forward to fall break at my school in east America. It is so cold, so I don't feel like going to school. I am so happy that we don't do a lot today, and are getting ready for the break. I like cooking on the break. I like making hot chocolate with marshmallows. I also like ham for Thanksgiving. What are your favorite foods? Also, if it gets super cold, I could make a snowman in front of my house. I love it also when we have no school when it snows really bad. What do you like to do? I want you to know that I care a lot about you. You are cared, and I enjoy writing these letters.
Dear friend, My name is Vy and i'm currently lived in Raleigh North Carolina. I'm a 16 years old boy but there is nothing special about me, i'm just an average guy who go to school to learn, but mostly to hang out with my friends. To me, friend is someone who play with you, help you, and fixed you when you do something wrong. Sometimes you guy might get in a fight, but that's what friend is all about. They are the one that will always be on your side no matter what, and they are always there when you needed them the most, or when you are in your toughest condition. But friendship won't last forever, there will be time where you guy have to be separate due to various reason like moving, or transferring. Therefore you should be thankful to your friend, those who are always on your side when you needed them the most before it's too late. I'm Vy, and i want to be that friend of your, and i can't wait to shared you more of my opinion, and my everyday experience.
